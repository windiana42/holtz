Introduction

Welcome to Holtz!

Holtz: The Game

Holtz is a board game similar sophisticated than chess. It is meant for two players but is also funny with three. The players alternately set or knock out stones according to several rules and have to get a certain amount of white, gray or black stones to win.

Holtz: The Software

The Holtz program supports playing Holtz offline against human or computer players, or on any network (e.g. the Internet).
There are many hints and help texts for beginners.
